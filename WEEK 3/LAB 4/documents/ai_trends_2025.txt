AI Trends in 2025
==================

1. Multimodal Models
Large language models now natively handle text, images, audio, and video in a single architecture.
Companies like OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic have shipped production-grade multimodal APIs.

2. Agent Frameworks
Autonomous agents that plan, use tools, and self-correct have moved from research to production.
MCP (Model Context Protocol) has emerged as a leading standard for agent-tool integration.

3. On-Device AI
Small language models (1-7B parameters) run efficiently on consumer laptops and phones.
Privacy-preserving inference without cloud round-trips is now mainstream.

4. Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
RAG pipelines are the dominant pattern for grounding LLM outputs in fresh or private data.
Vector databases like Pinecone, Weaviate, and pgvector are widely adopted.

5. AI Safety & Alignment
Constitutional AI and RLHF variants are standard training practices.
Governments in the EU and US have enacted AI regulation frameworks.
